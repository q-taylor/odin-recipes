Honey Lime Broccoli

Roasted Broccoli with the sweetness of Honey, tanginess of lime, and savory flavors of garlic and roasating!

Ingredients

  • Broccoli Florets frozen or fresh
  • Olive Oil
  • Honey
  • Lime Zest from 2 limes
  • Lime Juice
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • 2 Garlic Cloves - minced

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Pour 2 tablespoons Olive Oil over broccoli florets in bowl
  3. Salt and Pepper to taste
  4. Toss Broccoli to mix
  5. Put parchment paper or aluminum foil on baking sheet
  6. Pour Broccoli into lined baking sheet, spread evenly
  7. Combine the following in bowl and mix well:
    • Honey
    • Lime Zest
    • Minced Garlic
    • Lime Juice
    • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  8. When Broccoli is tender and browning on edges remove from oven (approximately 20 minutes)
  9. Toss Broccoli in bowl with honey lime mixture
  10. Om nom nom!
