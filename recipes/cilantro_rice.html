Cilantro Rice

How to Make Cilantro Rice Yummy yum Style

This recipe for cilantro rice is quite delicious. It uses lime, cilantro, garlic, and scallions to create a dish worth having!

Ingredients
  • rice olive oil cilantro lime zest lime juice scallions 1 clove of garlic
  1. Cook 1 cup of rice according to directions While rice is cooking
    • Zest 2 limes - set aside for juicing Mince garlic Chop 2-3 scallions Rough chop a handful of cilantro
    When rice is done cooking add lime zest, minced garlic, scallions, and 1 tablespoon of olive oil immediately and mix After rice has cooled for a couple minutes squeeze juice from the 2 limes into rice and add cilantro Mix and serve
