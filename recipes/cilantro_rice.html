Cilantro Rice

How to Make Cilantro Rice Yummy yum Style

This recipe for cilantro rice is quite delicious. It uses lime, cilantro, garlic, and scallions to create a dish worth having!

Ingredients

  • rice
  • olive oil
  • cilantro
  • lime zest
  • lime juice
  • 2-3 scallions
  • 1 clove of garlic
  1. Cook 1 cup of rice according to directions
  2. While rice is cooking
    • Zest 2 limes - set aside for juicing
    • Mince garlic
    • Chop scallions
    • Rough chop a handful of cilantro
  3. When rice is done cooking add lime zest, minced garlic, scallions, and 1 tablespoon of olive oil immediately and mix
  4. After rice has cooled for a couple minutes squeeze juice from the 2 limes into rice and add cilantro
  5. Mix and serve
