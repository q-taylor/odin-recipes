Soy Honey Salmon

Slightly spicy, sweet, tangy and delicious recipe for Salmon fillets!

Ingredients

  • 12 oz of Salmon Fillet
  • Olive Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Honey
  • Minced Ginger
  • 4 cloves Minced Garlic
  • Red Pepper Flakes

Steps:

  1. Make Marinade:
    • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
    • 1/3 cup Soy Sauce
    • 1/3 cup Honey
    • Ginger
    • Garlic
    • Red Pepper Flakes to taste
  2. Put Salmon in bag or bowl
  3. Pour half of marinade over Salmon and let rest in refridgerator for at least 30 minutes
  4. Heat olive oil in pan over medium high heat
  5. Cook Salmon Fillets skin side down for 2-3 minutes
  6. Flip Salmon and cook for an addition 1-2 minutes or until cooked through
  7. Remove Salmon from pan and add remaining marinade
  8. Reduce marinade into glaze
